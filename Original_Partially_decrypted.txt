EASUE WRDLRTEST PESE RYUE WRYWEIMEA AT WAUWRUATIYX AEMIWET. TIYWE AYWIEYT TIDET, TIDLUE DAYRAU AEMIWET UIKE TBE AFAWRT AIAEA LERLUE IY ARIYX WAUWRUATIRYT. EASUE IY TBE IYARTTSIAU SEMRURTIRY, TRDE DEWBAYIWAU AEMIWET PESE FRIUT TR ARTRDATE URYX TEAIRRT TATKT, TRWB AT XRIAIYX LATTESYT QRS URRDT. DRSE TRLBITTIWATEA EUEWTSIWAU DAWBIYET AIA TLEWIAUIOEA AYAURX WAUWRUATIRYT IY TBE EASUE 20TB WEYTRSE. TBE QISTT AIXITAU EUEWTSRYIW WAUWRUATIYX DAWBIYET PESE AEMEURLEA ARSIYX PRSUA PAS II. TBE QISTT TEDIWRYARWTRS TSAYTITTRST IY TBE UATE 1940T PESE QRUURPEA FE TBE TIUIWRY-FATEA DRTQET (DRT TSAYTITTRS) AYA DRYRUITBIW IYTEXSATEA WISWRIT (IW) WBIL TEWBYRURXIET IY TBE UATE 1950T, UEAAIYX TR TBE DIWSRLSRWETTRS AYA TBE DIWSRWRDLRTES SEMRURTIRY IY TBE 1970T. EYIAW PAT TBE QISTT EUEWTSRYIW, TRSIYX-WRDLUETE AEMIWE, AYA LESQRSDEA FAUUITTIWT TSAZEWTRSE WAUWRUATIRYT QRS TBE RYITEA TTATET ASDE.
TBE EYIAW[36] (EUEWTSRYIW YRDESIWAU IYTEXSATRS AYA WRDLRTES) PAT TBE QISTT EUEWTSRYIW LSRXSADDAFUE WRDLRTES FRIUT IY TBE R.T. AUTBRRXB TBE EYIAW PAT TIDIUAS TR TBE WRURTTRT, IT PAT DRWB QATTES, DRSE QUEHIFUE, AYA IT PAT TRSIYX-WRDLUETE. UIKE TBE WRURTTRT, A "LSRXSAD" RY TBE EYIAW PAT AEQIYEA FE TBE TTATET RQ ITT LATWB WAFUET AYA TPITWBET, A QAS WSE QSRD TBE TTRSEA LSRXSAD EUEWTSRYIW DAWBIYET TBAT WADE UATES. RYWE A LSRXSAD PAT PSITTEY, IT BAA TR FE DEWBAYIWAUUE TET IYTR TBE DAWBIYE PITB DAYRAU SETETTIYX RQ LURXT AYA TPITWBET. TBE LSRXSADDEST RQ TBE EYIAW PESE TIH PRDEY, RQTEY KYRPY WRUUEWTIMEUE AT TBE "EYIAW XISUT".[37][38]


IT WRDFIYEA TBE BIXB TLEEA RQ EUEWTSRYIWT PITB TBE AFIUITE TR FE LSRXSADDEA QRS DAYE WRDLUEH LSRFUEDT. IT WRRUA AAA RS TRFTSAWT 5000 TIDET A TEWRYA, A TBRRTAYA TIDET QATTES TBAY AYE RTBES DAWBIYE. IT AUTR BAA DRARUET TR DRUTILUE, AIMIAE, AYA TJRASE SRRT. BIXB TLEEA DEDRSE PAT UIDITEA TR 20 PRSAT (AFRRT 80 FETET). FRIUT RYAES TBE AISEWTIRY RQ ZRBY DARWBUE AYA Z. LSETLES EWKEST AT TBE RYIMESTITE RQ LEYYTEUMAYIA, EYIAW'T AEMEURLDEYT AYA WRYTTSRWTIRY UATTEA QSRD 1943 TR QRUU RLESATIRY AT TBE EYA RQ 1945. TBE DAWBIYE PAT BRXE, PEIXBIYX 30 TRYT, RTIYX 200 KIURPATTT RQ EUEWTSIW LRPES AYA WRYTAIYEA RMES 18,000 MAWRRD TRFET, 1,500 SEUAET, AYA BRYASEAT RQ TBRRTAYAT RQ SETITTRST, WALAWITRST, AYA IYARWTRST.[39]

TBE LSIYWILUE RQ TBE DRAESY WRDLRTES PAT LSRLRTEA FE AUAY TRSIYX IY BIT TEDIYAU 1936 LALES,[40] RY WRDLRTAFUE YRDFEST. TRSIYX LSRLRTEA A TIDLUE AEMIWE TBAT BE WAUUEA "RYIMESTAU WRDLRTIYX DAWBIYE" AYA TBAT IT YRP KYRPY AT A RYIMESTAU TRSIYX DAWBIYE. TBE QRYAADEYTAU WRYWELT RQ TRSIYX'T AETIXY IT TBE TTRSEA LSRXSAD, PBESE AUU TBE IYTTSRWTIRYT QRS WRDLRTIYX ASE TTRSEA IY DEDRSE. MRY YERDAYY AWKYRPUEAXEA TBAT TBE WEYTSAU WRYWELT RQ TBE DRAESY WRDLRTES PAT ARE TR TBIT LALES.[41] TRSIYX DAWBIYET ASE TR TBIT AAE A WEYTSAU RFZEWT RQ TTRAE IY TBERSE RQ WRDLRTATIRY. ZRBY MRY YERDAYY AT TBE RYIMESTITE RQ LEYYTEUMAYIA AUTR WISWRUATEA BIT QISTT ASAQT RQ A SELRST RY TBE EAMAW IY 1945.[19]


TBE DAYWBETTES FAFE PAT TBE PRSUA'T QISTT TTRSEA-LSRXSAD WRDLRTES. IT PAT FRIUT AT TBE MIWTRSIA RYIMESTITE RQ DAYWBETTES FE QSEAESIW W. PIUUIADT, TRD KIUFRSY AYA XERQQ TRRTIUU, AYA SAY ITT QISTT LSRXSAD RY 21 ZRYE 1948.[42] IT PAT AETIXYEA AT A TETTFEA QRS TBE PIUUIADT TRFE, TBE QISTT SAYARD-AWWETT AIXITAU TTRSAXE AEMIWE.[43] AUTBRRXB TBE WRDLRTES PAT WRYTIAESEA "TDAUU AYA LSIDITIME" FE TBE TTAYAASAT RQ ITT TIDE, IT PAT TBE QISTT PRSKIYX DAWBIYE TR WRYTAIY AUU RQ TBE EUEDEYTT ETTEYTIAU TR A DRAESY EUEWTSRYIW WRDLRTES.[44] AT TRRY AT TBE FAFE BAA AEDRYTTSATEA TBE QEATIFIUITE RQ ITT AETIXY, A LSRZEWT PAT IYITIATEA AT TBE RYIMESTITE TR AEMEURL IT IYTR A DRSE RTAFUE WRDLRTES, TBE DAYWBETTES DASK 1. TBE DASK 1 IY TRSY JRIWKUE FEWADE TBE LSRTRTELE QRS TBE QESSAYTI DASK 1, TBE PRSUA'T QISTT WRDDESWIAUUE AMAIUAFUE XEYESAU-LRSLRTE WRDLRTES.[45] FRIUT FE QESSAYTI, IT PAT AEUIMESEA TR TBE RYIMESTITE RQ DAYWBETTES IY QEFSRASE 1951. UERYT & WRDLAYE AEWIAEA TR TAKE AY AWTIME SRUE IY LSRDRTIYX TBE WRDDESWIAU AEMEURLDEYT RQ WRDLRTEST. FILRUAS ZRYWTIRY TSAYTITTRS (FZT).
TBE WRYWELT RQ A QIEUA-EQQEWT TSAYTITTRS PAT LSRLRTEA FE ZRUIRT EAXAS UIUIEYQEUA IY 1925. ZRBY FASAEEY AYA PAUTES FSATTAIY, PBIUE PRSKIYX RYAES PIUUIAD TBRWKUEE AT FEUU UAFT, FRIUT TBE QISTT PRSKIYX TSAYTITTRS, TBE LRIYT-WRYTAWT TSAYTITTRS, IY 1947, PBIWB PAT QRUURPEA FE TBRWKUEE'T FILRUAS ZRYWTIRY TSAYTITTRS IY 1948.[48][49] QSRD 1955 RYPASAT, TSAYTITTRST SELUAWEA MAWRRD TRFET IY WRDLRTES AETIXYT, XIMIYX SITE TR TBE "TEWRYA XEYESATIRY" RQ WRDLRTEST. WRDLASEA TR MAWRRD TRFET, TSAYTITTRST BAME DAYE AAMAYTAXET: TBEE ASE TDAUUES, AYA SEJRISE UETT LRPES TBAY MAWRRD TRFET, TR XIME RQQ UETT BEAT. ZRYWTIRY TSAYTITTRST PESE DRWB DRSE SEUIAFUE TBAY MAWRRD TRFET AYA BAA URYXES, IYAEQIYITE, TESMIWE UIQE. TSAYTITTRSIOEA WRDLRTEST WRRUA WRYTAIY TEYT RQ TBRRTAYAT RQ FIYASE URXIW WISWRITT IY A SEUATIMEUE WRDLAWT TLAWE. BRPEMES, EASUE ZRYWTIRY TSAYTITTRST PESE SEUATIMEUE FRUKE AEMIWET TBAT PESE AIQQIWRUT TR DAYRQAWTRSE RY A DATT-LSRARWTIRY FATIT, PBIWB UIDITEA TBED TR A YRDFES RQ TLEWIAUITEA ALLUIWATIRYT.[50]


AT TBE RYIMESTITE RQ DAYWBETTES, A TEAD RYAES TBE UEAAESTBIL RQ TRD KIUFRSY AETIXYEA AYA FRIUT A DAWBIYE RTIYX TBE YEPUE AEMEURLEA TSAYTITTRST IYTTEAA RQ MAUMET.[51] TBEIS QISTT TSAYTITTRSITEA WRDLRTES AYA TBE QISTT IY TBE PRSUA, PAT RLESATIRYAU FE 1953, AYA A TEWRYA MESTIRY PAT WRDLUETEA TBESE IY ALSIU 1955. BRPEMES, TBE DAWBIYE AIA DAKE RTE RQ MAUMET TR XEYESATE ITT 125 KBO WURWK PAMEQRSDT AYA IY TBE WISWRITSE TR SEAA AYA PSITE RY ITT DAXYETIW ASRD DEDRSE, TR IT PAT YRT TBE QISTT WRDLUETEUE TSAYTITTRSIOEA WRDLRTES. TBAT AITTIYWTIRY XRET TR TBE BASPEUU WAAET RQ 1955,[52] FRIUT FE TBE EUEWTSRYIWT AIMITIRY RQ TBE ATRDIW EYESXE SETEASWB ETTAFUITBDEYT AT BASPEUU.[52][53]




DRTQET (DRT TSAYTITTRS), TBRPIYX XATE (X), FRAE (F), TRRSWE (T) AYA ASAIY (A) TESDIYAUT. TBE XATE IT TELASATEA QSRD TBE FRAE FE AY IYTRUATIYX UAEES (LIYK).
TBE DETAU–RHIAE–TIUIWRY QIEUA-EQQEWT TSAYTITTRS (DRTQET), AUTR KYRPY AT TBE DRT TSAYTITTRS, PAT IYMEYTEA FE DRBADEA D. ATAUUA AYA AAPRY KABYX AT FEUU UAFT IY 1959.[54] IT PAT TBE QISTT TSRUE WRDLAWT TSAYTITTRS TBAT WRRUA FE DIYIATRSITEA AYA DATT-LSRARWEA QRS A PIAE SAYXE RQ RTET.[50] PITB ITT BIXB TWAUAFIUITE,[55] AYA DRWB URPES LRPES WRYTRDLTIRY AYA BIXBES AEYTITE TBAY FILRUAS ZRYWTIRY TSAYTITTRST,[56] TBE DRTQET DAAE IT LRTTIFUE TR FRIUA BIXB-AEYTITE IYTEXSATEA WISWRITT.[57][58] IY AAAITIRY TR AATA LSRWETTIYX, IT AUTR EYAFUEA TBE LSAWTIWAU RTE RQ DRT TSAYTITTRST AT DEDRSE WEUU TTRSAXE EUEDEYTT, UEAAIYX TR TBE AEMEURLDEYT RQ DRT TEDIWRYARWTRS DEDRSE, PBIWB SELUAWEA EASUIES DAXYETIW-WRSE DEDRSE IY WRDLRTEST.[59] TBE DRTQET UEA TR TBE DIWSRWRDLRTES SEMRURTIRY,[60] AYA FEWADE TBE ASIMIYX QRSWE FEBIYA TBE WRDLRTES SEMRURTIRY.[61][62] TBE DRTQET IT TBE DRTT PIAEUE RTEA TSAYTITTRS IY WRDLRTEST,[63][64] AYA IT TBE QRYAADEYTAU FRIUAIYX FURWK RQ AIXITAU EUEWTSRYIWT.[65]


IYTEXSATEA WISWRITT
DAIY ASTIWUET: IYTEXSATEA WISWRIT AYA IYMEYTIRY RQ TBE IYTEXSATEA WISWRIT
QRSTBES IYQRSDATIRY: LUAYAS LSRWETT AYA DIWSRLSRWETTRS
TBE YEHT XSEAT AAMAYWE IY WRDLRTIYX LRPES WADE PITB TBE AAMEYT RQ TBE IYTEXSATEA WISWRIT (IW). TBE IAEA RQ TBE IYTEXSATEA WISWRIT PAT QISTT WRYWEIMEA FE A SAAAS TWIEYTITT PRSKIYX QRS TBE SREAU SAAAS ETTAFUITBDEYT RQ TBE DIYITTSE RQ AEQEYWE, XERQQSEE P.A. ARDDES. ARDDES LSETEYTEA TBE QISTT LRFUIW AETWSILTIRY RQ AY IYTEXSATEA WISWRIT AT TBE TEDLRTIRD RY LSRXSETT IY JRAUITE EUEWTSRYIW WRDLRYEYTT IY PATBIYXTRY, A.W. RY 7 DAE 1952.[66]


TBE QISTT PRSKIYX IWT PESE IYMEYTEA FE ZAWK KIUFE AT TEHAT IYTTSRDEYTT AYA SRFEST YREWE AT QAISWBIUA TEDIWRYARWTRS.[67] KIUFE SEWRSAEA BIT IYITIAU IAEAT WRYWESYIYX TBE IYTEXSATEA WISWRIT IY ZRUE 1958, TRWWETTQRUUE AEDRYTTSATIYX TBE QISTT PRSKIYX IYTEXSATEA EHADLUE RY 12 TELTEDFES 1958.[68] IY BIT LATEYT ALLUIWATIRY RQ 6 QEFSRASE 1959, KIUFE AETWSIFEA BIT YEP AEMIWE AT "A FRAE RQ TEDIWRYARWTRS DATESIAU ... PBESEIY AUU TBE WRDLRYEYTT RQ TBE EUEWTSRYIW WISWRIT ASE WRDLUETEUE IYTEXSATEA".[69][70] BRPEMES, KIUFE'T IYMEYTIRY PAT A BEFSIA IYTEXSATEA WISWRIT (BEFSIA IW), SATBES TBAY A DRYRUITBIW IYTEXSATEA WISWRIT (IW) WBIL.[71] KIUFE'T IW BAA EHTESYAU PISE WRYYEWTIRYT, PBIWB DAAE IT AIQQIWRUT TR DATT-LSRARWE.[72]


YREWE AUTR WADE RL PITB BIT RPY IAEA RQ AY IYTEXSATEA WISWRIT BAUQ A EEAS UATES TBAY KIUFE.[73] YREWE'T IYMEYTIRY PAT TBE QISTT TSRE DRYRUITBIW IW WBIL.[74][72] BIT WBIL TRUMEA DAYE LSAWTIWAU LSRFUEDT TBAT KIUFE'T BAA YRT. LSRARWEA AT QAISWBIUA TEDIWRYARWTRS, IT PAT DAAE RQ TIUIWRY, PBESEAT KIUFE'T WBIL PAT DAAE RQ XESDAYIRD. YREWE'T DRYRUITBIW IW PAT QAFSIWATEA RTIYX TBE LUAYAS LSRWETT, AEMEURLEA FE BIT WRUUEAXRE ZEAY BRESYI IY EASUE 1959. IY TRSY, TBE LUAYAS LSRWETT PAT FATEA RY TBE TIUIWRY TRSQAWE LATTIMATIRY AYA TBESDAU RHIAATIRY LSRWETTET AEMEURLEA FE DRBADEA ATAUUA AT FEUU UAFT IY TBE UATE 1950T.[75][76][77]


DRAESY DRYRUITBIW IWT ASE LSEARDIYAYTUE DRT (DETAU-RHIAE-TEDIWRYARWTRS) IYTEXSATEA WISWRITT, FRIUT QSRD DRTQETT (DRT TSAYTITTRST).[78] AQTES TBE QISTT DRTQET PAT IYMEYTEA FE DRBADEA ATAUUA AYA AAPRY KABYX AT FEUU UAFT IY 1959,[79] ATAUUA QISTT LSRLRTEA TBE WRYWELT RQ TBE DRT IYTEXSATEA WISWRIT IY 1960, QRUURPEA FE KABYX IY 1961, FRTB YRTIYX TBAT TBE DRT TSAYTITTRS'T EATE RQ QAFSIWATIRY DAAE IT RTEQRU QRS IYTEXSATEA WISWRITT.[50][80] TBE EASUIETT EHLESIDEYTAU DRT IW TR FE QAFSIWATEA PAT A 16-TSAYTITTRS WBIL FRIUT FE QSEA BEIDAY AYA TTEMEY BRQTTEIY AT SWA IY 1962.[81] XEYESAU DIWSREUEWTSRYIWT UATES IYTSRARWEA TBE QISTT WRDDESWIAU DRT IW IY 1964,[82] AEMEURLEA FE SRFEST YRSDAY.[81] QRUURPIYX TBE AEMEURLDEYT RQ TBE TEUQ-AUIXYEA XATE (TIUIWRY-XATE) DRT TSAYTITTRS FE SRFEST KESPIY, ARYAUA KUEIY AYA ZRBY TASAWE AT FEUU UAFT IY 1967, TBE QISTT TIUIWRY-XATE DRT IW PITB TEUQ-AUIXYEA XATET PAT AEMEURLEA FE QEAESIWR QAXXIY AT QAISWBIUA TEDIWRYARWTRS IY 1968.[83] TBE DRTQET BAT TIYWE FEWRDE TBE DRTT WSITIWAU AEMIWE WRDLRYEYT IY DRAESY IWT.[84]


TBE AEMEURLDEYT RQ TBE DRT IYTEXSATEA WISWRIT UEA TR TBE IYMEYTIRY RQ TBE DIWSRLSRWETTRS,[85][86] AYA BESAUAEA AY EHLURTIRY IY TBE WRDDESWIAU AYA LESTRYAU RTE RQ WRDLRTEST. PBIUE TBE TRFZEWT RQ EHAWTUE PBIWB AEMIWE PAT TBE QISTT DIWSRLSRWETTRS IT WRYTEYTIRRT, LASTUE ARE TR UAWK RQ AXSEEDEYT RY TBE EHAWT AEQIYITIRY RQ TBE TESD "DIWSRLSRWETTRS", IT IT UASXEUE RYAITLRTEA TBAT TBE QISTT TIYXUE-WBIL DIWSRLSRWETTRS PAT TBE IYTEU 4004,[87] AETIXYEA AYA SEAUIOEA FE QEAESIWR QAXXIY PITB BIT TIUIWRY-XATE DRT IW TEWBYRURXE,[85] AURYX PITB TEA BRQQ, DATATRTBI TBIDA AYA TTAYUEE DAORS AT IYTEU.[88][89] IY TBE EASUE 1970T, DRT IW TEWBYRURXE EYAFUEA TBE IYTEXSATIRY RQ DRSE TBAY 10,000 TSAYTITTRST RY A TIYXUE WBIL.[58]


TETTED RY A WBIL (TRWT) ASE WRDLUETE WRDLRTEST RY A DIWSRWBIL (RS WBIL) TBE TIOE RQ A WRIY.[90] TBEE DAE RS DAE YRT BAME IYTEXSATEA SAD AYA QUATB DEDRSE. IQ YRT IYTEXSATEA, TBE SAD IT RTRAUUE LUAWEA AISEWTUE AFRME (KYRPY AT LAWKAXE RY LAWKAXE) RS FEURP (RY TBE RLLRTITE TIAE RQ TBE WISWRIT FRASA) TBE TRW, AYA TBE QUATB DEDRSE IT RTRAUUE LUAWEA SIXBT YEHT TR TBE TRW, TBIT AUU ARYE TR IDLSRME AATA TSAYTQES TLEEAT, AT TBE AATA TIXYAUT ARY'T BAME TR TSAMEU URYX AITTAYWET. TIYWE EYIAW IY 1945, WRDLRTEST BAME AAMAYWEA EYRSDRRTUE, PITB DRAESY TRWT FEIYX TBE TIOE RQ A WRIY PBIUE AUTR FEIYX BRYASEAT RQ TBRRTAYAT RQ TIDET DRSE LRPESQRU TBAY EYIAW, IYTEXSATIYX FIUUIRYT RQ TSAYTITTRST, AYA WRYTRDIYX RYUE A QEP PATTT RQ LRPES.
